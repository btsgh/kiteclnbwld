# User Requirements

Build a website for CleanBowled, a vegetarian nutrition business. The business provides healthy vegetarian food options for a varied range of customers: customers who are looking at maintaining their health, athletes/sports-persons who need a protein-rich diet, folks recovering from injury, Postpartum Women and similar wellness-focused customers. The business offers doorstep deliveries, has products in existing organic outlets, and sets up quick pop-up shops during particular events. Assume any missing business details as needed and create a strong conversion-focused marketing website. Include a compelling home page with a hero section, clear explanation of the customer types served, featured nutrition-focused offerings, delivery and availability information, presence in organic outlets, pop-up shop mention, trust-building sections, and a contact or inquiry section. Use a fresh, modern, clean visual style that feels healthy, credible, and energetic. This version should lean toward everyday wellness and clean eating with bright, natural, nourishing visuals and an approachable premium tone.
